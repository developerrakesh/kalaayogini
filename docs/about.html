Kalaa Yogini is born purely out of devotion towards the “Divine Feminine” who is responsible for the very existance of this universe.

I, the founder and artist behind Kalaa Yogini, am a devotee of Devi Linga Bhairavi and show my love and gratitude towards her through my art.

How it all started?

Through sickness and suffering, when my whole world was falling apart, Devi was the only entity capable of reaching out to me and show me a way out. It is in her devotion that I began to heal. Naturally, all I did began to happen in her shadow.

For someone who was not confident to paint and make human figures, I was suddnely able to let go of the fears.

What are the products?

I usually work with watercolors and sometimes with acrylics. You can find a variety of paintings and their prints available here.
